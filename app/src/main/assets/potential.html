Which shows that potential difference is the line integral of electric field intensity

EQUIPOTENTIAL SURFACE:-An equipotential surface is a surface where electric potential remain same at all points.
Characterstics of equipotential surface:-
1.The work done in moving a charge in a equipotential surface is zero.
2.The electric potential lines are always perpendicular to the equipotential surface.
3.No two equipotential surface can cross each other.

Principle:- A VGG is based on two factors
1-corona discharge
2- uniform distribution of charge.
CORONA DISCHARGE :- The process by which the charge at pointed end of a conductor gets discharge is called corona Discharge
-->It is due to high charge density.
UNIFORM DISTRIBUTION OF CHARGE:- The charge collected by the collector comb is uniformly distributed on the metallic sphere surface.
WORKING :-
As the sprayer comb is connected high tension battery($10^4$V) the charge density at the comb becomes very high and starts discharge the charge through sharp edges(i.e corona discharge) This charge is sprayed over the rayon belt which moves between two pulleys, this collector comb collects the charge from the belt and transfer it to the metallic sphere.
The uniform distribution of charge takes place over the metallic sphere. A high electric potential is attained by the metallic sphere ($10^7$) such a voltage make a potential difference for a positive charge ion kept inside a discharge tube. The potential on the metallic sphere is
$V=1/{{4Πε_o}Q/R$
The charge ion walks by w=qv and takes a very high energy toward the target on the other end at tube. In this way the target gets destroyed.
